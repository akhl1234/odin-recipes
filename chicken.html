Chicken Fry Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken pieces
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the chicken by cleaning and cutting it into pieces.
  2. In a bowl, mix together flour, salt, black pepper, and paprika.
  3. In another bowl, beat the eggs.
  4. Dip each chicken piece into the beaten eggs, then coat it with the flour mixture.
  5. Heat vegetable oil in a frying pan over medium heat.
  6. Fry the coated chicken pieces in the hot oil until golden brown and cooked through.
  7. Remove the chicken from the oil and place it on a paper towel to drain excess oil.
  8. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce or side dishes.
