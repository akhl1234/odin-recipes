Delicious Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, cheese, sauce, and various other ingredients. It's a hearty and comforting meal that's perfect for feeding a crowd or enjoying as leftovers.

Ingredients

For the lasagna, you'll need:

  • 1 pound (450g) lasagna noodles
  • 15 ounces (425g) ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups (200g) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup (100g) grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef or Italian sausage (optional)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jar (24 ounces or 680g) marinara sauce
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces or 410g) diced tomatoes, drained
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
